Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

1. What is Street Bites Manila?

Street Bites Manila is a mobile application designed to help users find street food stalls in Manila, particularly around Intramuros.

2. How does Street Bites Manila work?

The app uses GPS to display nearby food stalls on a map, allowing users to find vendors, read reviews, and check real-time availability.

3. Who can use this app?

Anyone! It is useful for students, tourists, and locals looking for quick and affordable food options.

Using the App

4. How do I find a food stall near me?

Open the app, and it will use your GPS location to show nearby food stalls. You can search and filter results.

5. Can I leave reviews and ratings for food vendors?

Yes, users can share their experiences through reviews and ratings.

6. How do I know if a vendor is open?

Vendors update their status in real-time, so you can check availability before visiting.
